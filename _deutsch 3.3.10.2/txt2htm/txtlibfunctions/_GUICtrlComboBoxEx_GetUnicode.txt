###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetUnicode

###Description###

###Syntax###

_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetUnicode ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle des Controls
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
