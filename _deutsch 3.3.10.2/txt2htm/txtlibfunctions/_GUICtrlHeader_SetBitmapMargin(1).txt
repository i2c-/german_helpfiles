###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlHeader_SetBitmapMargin

###Description###

###Syntax###
#include <GuiHeader.au3>
_GUICtrlHeader_SetBitmapMargin ( $hWnd, $iWidth )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle des Controls
$iWidth
	Breite des Randes eines Bitmaps in Pixel
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
