###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlToolbar_SetHotImageList

###Description###

###Syntax###
#include <GuiToolBar.au3>
_GUICtrlToolbar_SetHotImageList($hWnd, $hImageList)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@

	Handle des Controls
$hImageList
	Handle der zu setzenden Imagelist
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
