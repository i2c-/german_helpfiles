###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetParentHandle

###Description###

###Syntax###
#include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetParentHandle($hWnd [, $hItem = 0])

###Parameters###

$hItem

@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
