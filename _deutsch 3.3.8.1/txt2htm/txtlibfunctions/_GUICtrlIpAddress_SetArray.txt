###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlIpAddress_SetArray

###Syntax###

_GUICtrlIpAddress_SetArray($hWnd, $aAddress)

###Parameters###

$aAddress

		[1] - 2. Adressteil

		[3] - 4. Adressteil
@@End@@
